Build a Space Biology Knowledge Engine

This project was developed as our submission for the NASA Space Apps Challenge 2025. Our mission is to make space biology research more accessible, searchable, and actionable for researchers, students, and space enthusiasts worldwide.

What We Built

The Space Biology Knowledge Engine is a comprehensive platform that aggregates, organizes, and makes NASA's space biology research accessible through modern web technologies and AI-powered search capabilities.
